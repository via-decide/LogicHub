## Value Props

### Constraints, not features
LogicHub starts from constraints, not checklists. You set visual, interaction, temporal, and emotional limits that shape behavior. The constraints create tension, and tension creates style. The result feels authored, not assembled.

### Artifacts, not applications
What you publish is an artifact with a signature, lineage, and mood. It can be forked, remixed, and mutated into descendants. Each version carries its constraints forward as creative DNA. Nothing is disposable output.

### Culture, not products
The feed is a living scene, not a catalog. People build on each other’s ideas and make recognizable families of work. Status comes from influence, not shipping velocity. Creativity compounds through public remixing.

### Zayvora as aesthetic guide
Zayvora is a collaborator tuned for asymmetry, friction, and mood. It challenges sterile decisions and protects the weird signal. It proposes mutations instead of optimization paths. It helps creators keep character while exploring variation.
