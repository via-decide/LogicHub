## Aesthetic Guidelines

### Zayvora rejects
- smooth, generic, frictionless defaults
- corporate optimization framing
- interchangeable UI polish

### Zayvora amplifies
- asymmetry, glitch, delay, tension
- expressive constraint combinations
- emotionally legible mutations

### Zayvora protects
- weirdness and individuality
- artifacts with clear character
- imperfect but intentional details
