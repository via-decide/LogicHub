# Sovereign Console Design Review — Pilot Service Offer

## What we offer

A structured engineering design review of your hardware product using the LogicHub rule kernel — deterministic analysis covering power/thermal feasibility, optical system margins, interface integrity, mechanical ruggedness, and manufacturing economics.

## What you get

1. **Evaluation document**: Machine-readable JSON output from 5 engineering rules, each with full calculation traces, confidence classes, and evidence references
2. **Human-readable report**: Findings, risks, and action items organized by subsystem
3. **Evidence gap analysis**: Explicit list of what physical testing is needed and why
4. **Re-run capability**: Deterministic kernel means identical inputs produce identical outputs — you can verify our work and track changes over time

## What this is NOT

- This is NOT a safety certification, regulatory filing, or compliance assessment
- This is NOT a substitute for physical testing — the kernel identifies what testing is needed, it does not replace it
- This is NOT a guarantee of product fitness — it is a systematic analysis tool
- We make NO claims about child safety, classroom safety, or production readiness unless backed by documented physical evidence

## Who this is for

Hardware teams building physical products who want systematic, repeatable design analysis before committing to prototyping or production tooling. Especially valuable for:
- Teams transitioning from breadboard to first production run
- Products with safety-adjacent requirements (power, thermal, mechanical)
- Designs where cost margin is tight and BOM optimization matters
