# Outreach Message Template

Use this template when reaching out to potential pilot review customers. Adapt the specifics to the prospect's product.

---

Subject: Structured hardware design review — pilot opportunity

Hi [name],

We are piloting a structured engineering design review service for hardware products. The review uses a deterministic rule kernel that analyzes power/thermal feasibility, interface integrity, mechanical ruggedness, and manufacturing economics from your design documents.

**What you get:**
- Systematic check of 5 engineering domains with full calculation traces
- Evidence gap analysis showing exactly what physical testing you need
- Machine-readable results you can re-run and verify yourself
- Human-readable report with findings organized by severity

**What this is not:**
- Not a safety certification or regulatory assessment
- Not a substitute for physical testing

We are offering the first 2 reviews at pilot pricing. If you have a product in the design-to-prototype transition and want a systematic analysis before committing to production tooling, I would be happy to share the scope document and intake form.

Best,
[sender]

- Do not claim certifications, endorsements, or regulatory standing we do not have
