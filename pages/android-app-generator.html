Frequently Asked Questions

What Android SDK versions are supported?

By default, our local compiler compiles with target SDK 34 (Android 14) and matches a minimum SDK 26 (Android 8.0) for broad backward compatibility.

Can I publish these apps to the Google Play Store?

Yes. The output package is a standard Android release package. You can sign it using your developer credentials and upload it directly to Google Play.
